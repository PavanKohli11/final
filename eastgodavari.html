Ainavilli

Description: The Sri Siddhi Vinayaka Swamy Temple in Ainavilli, East Godavari, is one of the most revered temples dedicated to Lord Ganesha.



Rajahmundry

Description: River Godavari flows through the west of Rajahmundry. The Rajahmundry traps, part of the Deccan Traps, are located on the Godavari River and are particularly interesting to geologists.



ISKCON Temple

The ISKCON Temple, Rajahmundry, is a peaceful and spiritually uplifting place that is perfect for meditation, devotion, and experiencing divine bliss.



Kotilingeshwara Temple

Description: The Kotilingeshwara Temple in Rajahmundry, East Godavari, is a famous Shiva temple situated on the banks of the Godavari River. It is one of the significant spiritual attractions in the region.



Papi Hills

Description: Papi Hills (Papi Kondalu) is a breathtaking hill range along the Godavari River, famous for its scenic beauty, boat cruises, and tribal culture. It is often compared to the Kashmir of South India due to its lush green hills, waterfalls, and peaceful surroundings.



Antarvedi

Description: Antarvedi Known for the Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple, located at the confluence of the Godavari River and the Bay of Bengal. Offers a unique Sagara Sangamam (Sea-River Confluence) experience.
